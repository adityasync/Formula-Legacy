# 8-Bit Driver Portrait Prompt Template

**Prompt used:**
> "8-bit pixel art portrait of Formula 1 driver {driver_name}, face only close up, {distinctive_features}, no helmet, smiling, trading card style, retro arcade colors, vibrant red and black background"

**Parameters:**
*   `{driver_name}`: Full name of the driver (e.g., "Lewis Hamilton")
*   `{distinctive_features}`: Optional tweaks (e.g., "blonde hair", "mustache", "vintage cap")

**Style Notes:**
*   Ensure "face only" to avoid helmet obstruction.
*   "Vibrant red and black background" enforces the F1Pedia color scheme.
*   "Trading card style" ensures a framed look.
